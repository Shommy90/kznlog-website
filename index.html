WHAT IS KZNLOG?

Software solutions for
complex logistic operations

KaizenLog is an open ecosystem for logistics contractual settlement based on a DLT protocol for the transfer of electronic/digital documents through the use of public/private key infrastructure and distributed infrastructure. It also envisions the integration of most logistics systems for command interactions through APIs. Ultimately a single window UX/UI solution that aggregates all logistics usages a client needs and insures interoperability between the systems. The chosen architecture for the system enables us the deployment of “computational smart contracts” for securing digital contractual assets. We connect the digital representation of assets with custody services of valuable documents through the use of open libraries of contractual assets.

WHY ARE WE BUILDING KZNLOG?

01

The logistics industry suffers from many inefciencies. The assessed amount of optimisation through a digital architecture is a 30% increase in efciency in a global market of 54 Trillion USD per year.

02

We have faced various bottle necks and red tapes from current age bureaucracy and the paper based economy. When information is travelling faster than the speed of light it cannot be that we still have to rely on the speed of a horse to conduct business.

03

We want to decrease the cost of transacting in a global economy, and build an ecosystem. For a more inclusive global business that is easier to manage than playing a video game.

04

KZNLOG is an abbreviation for Kaizen Logistics. A protocol for constantly upgraded business.
